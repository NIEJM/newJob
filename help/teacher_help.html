Q1: What type of class does Hiii-Class offer to students?

A1: Based on the age of the student, Hiii-Class provides On-door Teaching and Public Area Teaching options for the kids. On door teaching is only available for kids & youths between 3-14 years old, with the guardian at home during the class. The teaching venue can be (but not restricted to) the living room or study room. The Public Area Teaching option is available for all students including kids. The tutor will appoint his/her preferred teaching venue i.e. nearest cafe from home, or the community center. The students will travel to that appointed address for the face-to-face teaching.

Q2: How will I receive my payment?

A2: The tutor can request to withdraw payment anytime during the month and Hiii-Class will process the transactions at the beginning of every month (i.e. 1st of March) to the tutor’s default bank account.

Q3: How does the referral program work if I refer any friends?

A3: Hiii-Class always welcome new qualified and passionate tutors therefore we encourage our tutors to refer friends to join us. Once the tutor referred by you successfully passes the Hiii-Class interview, you will receive ￥200 bonus from us.

Q4: What does the order status "N" "G, N" "M" "G, M" mean as a part of the orders?

A4:
　　N - Means New 1 V 1 order without any time change
　　G, N - Means Group New order without any time change
　　M - Means Modified 1 V 1 order due to time change by student
　　G M - Means Group Modified order due to time change by student
